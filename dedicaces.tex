\chapter*{\Huge Dedication}
\raggedright

To my dearest parents,\textbf{\textit{Imen}} and \textbf{\textit{Meher}} the two pillars of my life. No words could ever fully capture the depth of my gratitude and love for you. Every step I take is built upon the foundation of your countless sacrifices, your endless patience, and your boundless love. You have given me everything without ever asking for anything in return your time and your energy. I am who I am today because of you. I dedicate this work to you with all my heart. May God protect you always and bless you with long, joyful, and peaceful lives. I hope making you always proud.

To my beloved brother, \textbf{\textit{Nizar}}, whose gentle presence and sincere care have always comforted me. Your a strong man with a heart of gold, and I am grateful for the bond we share. Thank you for being a source of support and understanding throughout this journey.

To my departed grandmother, \textbf{\textit{Turkeya}}, whose teached me the meaning of love, kindness and giving people without expecting anything in return. Your memory continues to inspire me every day, and I carry your love in my heart as I dedicate this work to you. may you rest in peace and may God bless you with the highest place in paradise.

To my best friend and like a brother, my companion throughout these five years of university, \textbf{\textit{Mouadh}}, thank you for always standing by my side, in both joy and hardship. Your friendship has been a blessing and your presence a source of balance and comfort. I dedicate a part of this achievement to the bond we share.

To my dear friend \textbf{\textit{Hadhami}}, who was like a big sister to me from preparing my internship to guiding me in Paris, your help, kindness, and support have meant more than words can express. I owe you so much, and I will always be grateful.

To my friend \textbf{\textit{Eya}}, whose guidance and care have lit many of my paths. Thank you for being there in difficult times and offering your wisdom and strength with a kind heart.

To my close friends \textbf{\textit{Chedi}}, \textbf{\textit{Wajih}} and \textbf{\textit{Hilali}}, whose friendship I will always cherish. Thank you for the laughter, the honesty, and the many shared memories. You have brought joy and energy to this journey, and I’m grateful to have walked it with you.

This work is lovingly dedicated to all of you with deep affection, lasting respect, and infinite gratitude.
